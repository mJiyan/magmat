Decibel Level Meter

Ever wondered how noisy your surroundings are? dbMeter allows you to measure the decibel levels of any environment with just a tap. Whether youre in a loud city street, a quiet library, or at a concert, this app provides real-time sound measurements, helping you stay informed about your auditory environment. Perfect for anyone concerned with noise pollution or for those simply curious about the world around them.

houseRule

The Ultimate Solution for Roommates

HouseRule is designed to make shared living easier and more organized. With HouseRule, roommates can set up a house rule system, manage shared expenses, and keep track of bill payments seamlessly. Even if they don 't speak the same language, the app offers a built-in multilingual chat feature, allowing for clear and easy communication. By scanning bills and keeping track of who has paid and who hasnt, HouseRule ensures transparency and fairness in every shared living arrangement.
